Odoo Marketplace Stripe Connect

Let your Odoo Marketplace customers pay smartly with Stripe!

Odoo Marketplace Stripe Connect allows you to integrate Stripe Payment Acquirer with your Odoo Marketplace. Hence, you and your sellers can accept the payments from your customers via Stripe Payment Acquirer.

Odoo Marketplace is one of the most trending marketplace platforms which offers you the complete flexibility to customize it as you want. As a Marketplace Owner (Admin), it gives you complete authority to approve/disapprove products, orders, sellers, etc, manage seller's payments, seller shops and much more.

Online Payments play a great role in any online store as it offers an ease to your customers. When talking about payment acquirers, Stripe is one of the payment acquirers that come first in everyone's mind.

”Millions of companies in over 120 countries use Stripe to start, run, and scale their businesses.” Stripe is one of the most leading payment acquirers worldwide which allows customers to pay via credit cards.

Integrating the Stripe Payment Acquirer with Odoo Marketplace!

Now, you can easily connect Stripe Payment Acquirer with your Odoo Marketplace for the ease of payments. The module lets your customers pay for their order via their credit card. Moreover, they can also save their card details for faster payments.

Detailed Features List

Integrate Odoo with Stripe Payment Gateway

  • Allows one-step integration of Odoo with Stripe Payment Gateway.
  • You can allow sellers to register themselves using stripe connect.
  • You can enable/disable payment method from admin panel

Redirect to Stripe portal for secure Online Payments

  • The module redirects the customers to the Stripe Portal to ensure secure checkout.
  • The payment is completed on Stripe Portal.
  • Once done, the customer is directed back to the Odoo Marketplace to continue shopping

Offers a secure and safe payment gateway on Odoo Marketplace

  • It offers complete security as it covers 100% PCI compliance.
  • Improves transparency and avoids fraud.
  • Helps in improving the security while making transactions with Stripe.

3D Secure enabled for enhanced security

  • The module comes with 3D-secure features enabled for Odoo Marketplace.
  • 3D secure enables a secondary security check to authenticate payer via OTP.
  • The feature makes the payment more secure as it involves double verification.

Manage Payment Transactions in Odoo backend

  • The Odoo admin can see the transaction details of the orders in the Odoo backend.
  • The order form shows the details of the mode of payment used by the customer to make the payment for his/her order.
  • The list of the transaction is also maintained in the Stripe merchant portal.

Save Customer Card details For Quicker Checkout

  • The customer can save their cards directly on Stripe Server.
  • The saved cards can be then used while making the payment on Odoo Marketplace.
  • It allows for a quicker checkout for customers on Odoo Marketplace.

Manage Refund directly from the stripe Account

  • Admin/Seller can manage refund directly from their stripe account

Manage Seller Payment Easily

  • Payment is splitted between the seller(s) and admin as set in the backend.
